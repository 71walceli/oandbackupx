<p>Fork of the infamous OAndBackup with the aim to bring the app to 2020, adding support for the newest versions of android and update it's look.</p>
<p>The app allows you to make and restore backups of the apps on your device. <i>OAndBackupX</i> enables you to save app data to a user-accessible location. Both backup / restore of single apps and of multiple apps are supported.</p>
<p><i>OAndBackupX</i> requires a working busybox installation at the moment (often pre-installed on custom roms).</p>
<p><br><b>Requires root:</b>  cause it copies files from /data/data, /data/app and /system/app.</p>
<p>Status: Beta</p>
